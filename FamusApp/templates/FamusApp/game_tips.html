Tips for Playing Pac-Man

Master Pac-Man with these top strategies to help you reach a higher score and avoid ghosts effectively!

  • Stay Calm: The game becomes challenging as you progress. Remain calm to make quick and accurate decisions.
  • Plan Your Route: Try to clear sections of dots in an efficient way, avoiding areas with high ghost activity.
  • Use Power Pellets Wisely: Save power pellets for when ghosts are closing in on you to maximize points and avoid getting cornered.
